Portacular Resonances


Portacular Resonances is a set of art-research experiments using radiophonics to explore sense, space, and affect. Using AM radio recievers tuned to 530khz, one is able to hear ambient electrical activities both natural and artificial. This noise becomes signal in the serendipitous searching for sonic artifact. Drawing on psychogeography, speculative design, process philosophy, and affect theory, Portacular Resonances has resulted in art installations, guided tours, talks, and writings. In my dissertation, portacular resonances occurs as a "machine for fighting anxiety".
